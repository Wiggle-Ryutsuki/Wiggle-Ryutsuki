My Hobbies

After a long day of drowning in study materials, I like to put everything aside and pull out a different kind of book â€“ my sketchbook.

I'm really into drawing; it's a big hobby of mine. I can't sit still for too long without doing something with my hands, preferably doing something creative. Even if I've spent the entire day jotting down notes, I just can't keep my hands down.

When I start sketching, it's like I enter a whole new world of frustrations. I'll sketch something, erase it, and then sketch it and erase it, and sketch again until the shapes looks just right. It might sound strange, trading a frustrating study session into a frustrating sketching, but there's something oddly satisfying about the whole process, and I wouldn't trade it for anything.

My biggest inspiration and style come from anime. Sometimes, I'll be watching an anime show while I draw. I'm just blown away by how beautiful and diverse the art styles are in anime. And when I see others recreating those styles, it makes me want to be a part of that creative community too.
