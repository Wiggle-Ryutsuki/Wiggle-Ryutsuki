My Passion

As a growing teenager I have yet to have a clear path in front of me. I'm still on the journey to discover myself. While many of my friends may already have a clear sense of their interests and hobbies that lead to clear paths to their future, I'm still exploring my options and experimenting.

And that's ok. I don't have to rush. I want to take my time and really want to find what clicks. Who knows what will work for me. I'm excited to find out what life has in store for me and what it leads me to.
